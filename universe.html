Kitty Woos Universe

Kitty Woos Universe is a digital universe ruled by Mr Kitty Woos,
and the first Kitty Woos Studios product.
It is part 3d, using Babylon.js for 3d scenes, and part 2d like most websites.
It has lots of 3d solar systems with planets orbiting stars.
The planets have unique expiriences, including
more 3d scenes, games, and more.
Kitty Woos Universe will be ready soon,
with lots of planets being added all the time.
