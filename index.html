About Mr. Kitty Woos

Mr. Kitty Woos is a toy cat living in Toronto. He is also trying to become the overlord of the universe, but he wants to rule the internet first. He now makes fun and interactive websites with his web development company, Kitty Woos Studios. Kitty Woos Studios is based in Toronto and has been making things for Mr. Kitty Woos since late 2024.

Kitty Woos Universe

Kitty Woos Universe is a digital universe made by Kitty Woos Studios and Mr. Kitty Woos. It is a very big project, so it may take some time before it gets put online.It is not released yet, but version one should launch sometime this year. Kitty Woos Universe will be an online universe filled with games and interactive solar systems, with each planet as its own game. It will also feature 3d scenes and physics demonstrations.

Kitty Woos CLI

Kitty Woos CLI is a virtual Command Line Interface made by Kitty Woos Studios and Mr. Kitty Woos that has custom commands that can control APIs and control the website. It is not online yet, but it should come out later this year.
